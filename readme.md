[The RWTH Center for Computational Life Sciences](https://www.ccls.rwth-aachen.de/go/id/ystdj/?lidx=1) strengthens and connects research activities in Computational Life Sciences at RWTH Aachen University.


This is a curated list of awesome libraries, projects, and other resources developed at the CCLS. Contributions are welcome!

## Software

- [MxlPy](https://github.com/Computational-Biology-Aachen/MxlPy) - enables easily building mechanistic learning models <img src="https://img.shields.io/github/stars/Computational-Biology-Aachen/MxlPy?style=social" align="center">
- [MxlBricks](https://github.com/Computational-Biology-Aachen/mxl-bricks) - combines reactions bricks to quickly build larger models  <img src="https://img.shields.io/github/stars/Computational-Biology-Aachen/mxl-bricks?style=social" align="center">
- [MxlWeb](https://github.com/Computational-Biology-Aachen/mxl-web) - runs ODE models natively in the browser  <img src="https://img.shields.io/github/stars/Computational-Biology-Aachen/mxl-web?style=social" align="center">
- [PySBML](https://github.com/Computational-Biology-Aachen/pysbml) - takes SBML models and makes them simpler <img src="https://img.shields.io/github/stars/Computational-Biology-Aachen/pysbml?style=social" align="center">
